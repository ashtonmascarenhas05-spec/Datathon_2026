## KSP Crime Intelligence Command Centre
---
Problem Statement: Advanced Data Visualization &amp; AI Driven Crime Analytics Platform
--- 
The KSP Crime Intelligence Command Center (KSP-CICC) is envisioned as the analytical nerve center for Karnataka State Police — a unified, serverless, AI-augmented platform that converts fragmented, station-level crime records into district-wide, real-time operational intelligence. The vision is to shift policing posture from reactive record-keeping to proactive, evidence-driven deployment of resources, without requiring officers to interpret raw data themselves.
